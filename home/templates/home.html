About Me :)

I'm a self-taught visual artist who's always been drawn to capturing feelings through color and movement. Painting started as a way for me to make sense of my thoughts, and what's happening around me. Over time it became my favorite way to tell stories without words. A lot of my work explores the relationship between people and music — the way sound can shape emotions, memories, and moments.

I've been creating art for over four years, and for me, it's always been about connection. I love making pieces that draw people in and make them pause for a moment — to feel something, reflect, or start a conversation.
